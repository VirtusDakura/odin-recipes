Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

A rich and velvety tomato soup infused with the fresh, herbaceous aroma of basil. This comforting classic is topped with a swirl of cream and served alongside crusty bread—perfect for a cozy evening.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 4 cups ripe tomatoes, chopped (or 2 cans of diced tomatoes)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Sauté the onion and garlic until softened.
  2. Add the tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour in the vegetable broth and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  5. Stir in the heavy cream and chopped basil leaves.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and sugar if needed.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve hot with crusty bread.
