Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

A delectable Italian pasta dish with creamy, savory sauce, crispy pancetta, and a generous sprinkle of Parmesan. This timeless recipe is simple yet flavorful, ideal for a hearty and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 400g (14 oz) spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs + 2 egg yolks
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 150g (5 oz) pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed (optional)
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, egg yolks, and Parmesan cheese. Set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook the pancetta until crispy. Add garlic if using and saut√© for 1 minute.
  4. Add the hot spaghetti to the pan with the pancetta and toss to combine.
  5. Remove the pan from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with parsley and additional Parmesan, if desired. Serve immediately.
