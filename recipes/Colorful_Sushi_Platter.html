Colorful Sushi Platter

An artistic array of sushi rolls crafted with fresh ingredients like salmon, avocado, and cucumber. Served with classic condiments—wasabi, pickled ginger, and soy sauce—this dish is a perfect blend of flavor and elegance.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed) per roll
  • 100g (3.5 oz) fresh salmon, sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • Soy sauce for dipping
  • Pickled ginger and wasabi for serving
  • Optional toppings: sesame seeds, spicy mayo

Steps

  1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Cook the rice with water in a rice cooker or on the stovetop. Let it cool slightly.
  3. In a small bowl, mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Fold it into the cooked rice.
  4. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving 1 inch at the top.
  5. Add your fillings, such as salmon, avocado, and cucumber, in a line near the bottom edge.
  6. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat and seal the edge with a bit of water.
  7. Slice the roll into pieces with a sharp knife. Repeat for additional rolls.
  8. Arrange the sushi on a platter and serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.
